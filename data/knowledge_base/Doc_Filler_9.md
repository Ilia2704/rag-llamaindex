 Clean Desk Policy (CDP) Guidelines for RoboTech Employees
---------------------------------------------------------

### Introduction

In the interest of maintaining a secure and organized work environment, RoboTech enforces a _Clean Desk Policy_ (CDP). This policy is designed to minimize potential risks associated with confidential information being left unattended on desks.

### Scope

The CDP applies to all employees within the company, regardless of their position or work location. It is essential that every employee understands and adheres to this policy to ensure the safety and integrity of our data and intellectual property.

### Key Requirements

1. **Clear Desk at End of Day**: At the end of each workday, employees must clear their desks of all confidential documents and materials. This includes papers, laptops, mobile devices, and any other items that may contain sensitive information.

2. **Lockable Storage**: If confidential documents or materials cannot be taken home, they should be securely stored in lockable drawers or cabinets at the end of each day.

3. **Regular Cleaning**: Employees are encouraged to maintain a clean and organized workspace throughout the day to minimize clutter and potential security risks. Regular cleaning is also performed by facility management during off-hours.

4. **Compliance with External Regulations**: RoboTech complies with all relevant local, national, and international regulations regarding data privacy and security. Employees are expected to adhere to these regulations in their daily work activities, including when it comes to the CDP.

### Consequences of Non-Compliance

Non-compliance with the Clean Desk Policy may result in disciplinary action, as outlined in the company's Employee Handbook. It is essential that all employees take their responsibility regarding data security seriously and adhere to this policy to protect both themselves and the company.
